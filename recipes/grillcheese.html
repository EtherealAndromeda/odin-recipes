Sweet n Spicy Grilled Cheese

Here is how i make a personally weird kind of grilled cheese.

Unlike the image, this recipe actually does not use orange cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • As many slices of pepper jack cheese as you see fit
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 Tsp Honey
  • 4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • Unsalted Butter

Directions

  1. Place the cheese slices between the two slices of bread
  2. Melt butter in the skillet and place sandwhich in skillet. Toast for 45 seconds, add half the Honey and Cayenne, then turn over.
  3. Season sandwhich with salt and pepper, cook for 45 more seconds before adding the remaining Honey and Cayenne, then turning it over again.
  4. Continue to cook on each side in 45-60 second increments until the bread and cheese are cooked to your liking.

Unconventional style, but I swear it's so good.
