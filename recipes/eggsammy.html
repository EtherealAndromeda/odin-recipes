Scrambled Egg Sandwhich

Yeah I know this one is really weird lol, I concocted this as a kid and sometimes i go back to it out of nostalgia.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Butter
  • 1/4thC whole milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Fruit Jam of choice (Ive always liked seedless Blueberry Jam)

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and combine with salt and milk, whisk with fork until combined well
  2. Melt butter into a pan on medium heat and pour in egg mixture, cook to desired consistency while sitrring constantly
  3. Remove pan from heat. Toast the bread slices lightly and spread fruit Jam on both peices.
  4. Assemble sandwhich by spreading eggs onto the jam and topping it with the 2nd slice, jam side down.
